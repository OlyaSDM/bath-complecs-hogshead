About us

History of the complex

Only here you will feel what a real bath is. After a busy working week, when you feel the need to have a good rest, relieve accumulated fatigue and routine worries, the "Hogshead" bath complex will open the doors to the delightful world of unsurpassed vip saunas and baths among the forest and crystal clear lake.

The Austrian city of Sölden is famous not only for ski slopes, but also for the general complex. Here you can completely relax, get away from everyday problems, plunge into the birth of bliss. You must appreciate the ancient traditions of the baths of the five peoples of the world, learn the mystery of the complete fulfillment of the soul and body. Nature itself takes care of your health.

Our services

Description of procedures

"Relax"

In this soaring, fragrant meadow hay is used. The fragrance of the herbs of a summer meadow helps to plunge into serenity and relieve accumulated fatigue

"Cedar"

Cedar paws combined with different herbs are steamed and laid out on the shelves. This procedure is especially useful in the treatment of colds

"White bath"

A real stove works in the bath in white. Several stones either simply lie on the stove, or in a special compartment above the firebox. The smoke from the stove immediately goes into the chimney

"SPA procedures"

SPA procedures are a sure way to prolong youth and preserve beauty. Natural ingredients normalize metabolic processes in the body

Complex "Carnation"

Fragrant flowers are laid out on the shelves. The procedure perfectly strengthens the nervous system, immunity

"Forest spirit"

Eucalyptus, linden, birch and oak brooms are steamed and laid out on the shelves. Nature itself takes care of your health

"Black bath"

The main difference between this type of bath and the previous one is the absence of a chimney from the stove. When the stove is heated, the smoke does not come out of the room to the outside, but remains inside, warming it up. As a result, the inner surfaces of the steam room are covered with black soot and soot

Massage with brooms

Brooms emit phytoncides, that kill microbes. Essential oils that improve metabolism and rejuvenate. The body seems to wake up after a long sleep, such a state cannot but rejoice
